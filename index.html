Threads of One Fabric

By Kallol Chakrabarti

A Journey into Hindu Unity for Children

About the Book

Threads of One Fabric is an inspiring children's book authored by Kallol Chakrabarti, designed to teach young readers about the beauty of Hindu unity and the diverse yet interconnected traditions within Hinduism. Through engaging stories and vibrant illustrations, children will learn how different practices and beliefs form a single, beautiful fabric of faith.

Why This Book?

  • Promotes cultural awareness and religious harmony.
  • Easy-to-understand language for children.
  • Encourages values of respect, inclusion, and unity.
